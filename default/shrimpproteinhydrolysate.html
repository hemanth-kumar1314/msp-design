Shrimp Protein Hydrolysate (Paste & Powder)

Shrimp Protein Hydrolysate is a highly bioavailable protein source derived from shrimp processing materials through enzymatic hydrolysis. This process breaks down proteins into small peptides and free amino acids.

Available in both a concentrated Paste and an easily mixable Powder form, the Hydrolysate is an elite feed additive and ingredient. It is highly valued for its exceptional digestibility, rapid absorption, and intense palatability.

It is a critical component for starter feeds, high-performance aquaculture diets, and specialty pet foods, ensuring rapid growth and superior nutrient utilization in juvenile and high-stress animals.

Applications & Key Benefits

Feed Palatability Booster

The high concentration of free amino acids (especially Glutamate) provides a powerful Umami and attractant signal, ensuring rapid feed intake and reduced waste.

Growth Promotion

The Chitin content in shrimp meals is believed to have a significant growth-promoting effect on farmed fish and shrimp.

Nutraceutical Potential

Contains bioactive peptides that may possess antioxidant, anti-hypertensive, or immune-modulating properties, adding health value beyond basic nutrition.

Flexible Formulation

The Paste form is excellent for liquid feed coatings and supplements, while the Powder is perfect for dry mix inclusion in pellets and crumbles.
