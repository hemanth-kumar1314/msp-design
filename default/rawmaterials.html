Sustainable Sourcing: From Waste to High-Value Ingredients

At MSP, we operate our own dedicated procurement facility, combining innovation and science to develop value-added products.

By combining innovation, scientific research, and a strong commitment to sustainability, we focus on upcycling marine waste into high-quality ingredients that serve various industries, including Food & Beverages, Agriculture, Animal Feed, Pharmaceuticals, and more.
