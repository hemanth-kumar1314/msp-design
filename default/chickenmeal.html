Premium Chicken Meal (Poultry By-Product Meal)

We produce superior quality Chicken Meal (Poultry By-Product Meal), a highly digestible and nutrient-dense protein source for the animal feed industry. It's rendered from clean, wholesome poultry parts, excluding feathers, heads, and feet, ensuring a consistent and high-quality ingredient.

Our Chicken Meal is an essential component in aquaculture, poultry, swine, and pet food formulations, providing a concentrated source of protein, essential amino acids, and valuable minerals like phosphorus and calcium.

The rendering process is controlled to maximize bioavailability and maintain freshness, making it a cost-effective alternative to other protein meals without compromising dietary performance.

Applications & Key Benefits

Fat/Oil Content

Low to moderate fat levels (typically 8% to 15%), providing an excellent source of energy and essential fatty acids.

Essential Feed Supplement

Widely incorporated into premixes for Poultry, Swine, Cattle, and Aquatic Feeds to ensure optimal mineral levels, significantly improving feed conversion ratio (FCR).

Moisture & Ash

Controlled moisture content to ensure extended shelf life and low Ash levels (typically below 18%) for superior quality.
