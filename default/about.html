About

Home / About

Innovative Valorization of Marine Residuals into Premium Components.

MSP Redefining the Future of Functional Marine Ingredients.

At MSP, we believe that sustainability begins at the source. We are a purpose-driven ingredient manufacturing company committed to upcycling seafood waste into high-quality, food-grade products for the food, pet food, and nutraceutical industries.

Our Mission

MSP seeks to deliver the highest quality seafood products at competitive prices while maintaining long-term relationships with our customers and our suppliers. We strive to source products from only the most environmentally and socially responsible supply partners..

Zero-Waste Foundation

Our operations are rooted in a zero-waste philosophy, where every input is utilized, and nothing goes to waste. We integrate innovation in circular economy into every step, ensuring we protect and sustain the marine environment.

We ensure consistency, traceability, purity, and safety by sourcing responsibly from verified local partners.

Crustacean By-products

Sourced from verified, local seafood processing partners, such as Jagadeesh Marine in Bhimavaram, who provide us with traceable shrimp shells, heads, and other crustacean by-products.

Freshwater Fish Scales

For our fish-based ingredients, we use only freshwater fish scales from Rohu, Catla, and Tilapia, ensuring batch consistency, purity, and safety.

Crab By-products

We responsibly procure crab shells and residual parts from dedicated suppliers, recovering essential biopolymers like Chitin and Chitosan for diverse industrial applications.

Science & High-Value Ingredients

Raw materials, once considered waste, are transformed in our facilities using advanced enzymatic and biotechnological processes under strict quality control.

— Shrimp Protein Hydrolysate & Oil
— Fish Collagen & Gelatin
— Biopolymer Chitin
— Nutraceutical Ingredients
