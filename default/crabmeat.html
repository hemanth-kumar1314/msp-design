Crab Meat

Crab Meat is a highly sought-after product derived from the edible muscle tissue of high-quality crabs. It is recognized globally for its delicate flavor, tender texture, and superior nutritional profile.

We process and prepare our crab meat to ensure the utmost freshness and hygiene, offering both pasteurized and frozen forms suitable for diverse markets. It is naturally high in protein, Vitamin B12, and Omega-3 fatty acids, while being very low in fat.

As a versatile product, it serves both the premium food service industry and as a high-value ingredient in specialized food formulations, including gourmet pet and aquatic feeds where high palatability is essential.

Applications & Key Benefits

Premium Food Ingredient

Ideal for the HORECA (Hotel, Restaurant, Catering) sector and retail as a ready-to-use, high-end seafood product in salads, bisques, and appetizers.

Exceptional Protein Source

Provides a complete amino acid profile and is extremely lean, making it an excellent, healthy protein boost for any diet or feed formulation.

Natural Flavor Attractant

The intense, savory flavor is a powerful natural attractant for premium fish and shrimp feed, maximizing feed consumption and minimizing waste.

Nutrient Density

Rich in essential micronutrients, including Selenium, Copper, and Zinc, which support immune function and overall physiological health.
