Fish Meal & OiL

Fish Meal is a protein-rich, highly digestible supplement produced by cooking, pressing, drying, and grinding whole fish or fish offcuts. Fish Oil is the nutrient-dense lipid byproduct, rich in essential Omega-3 fatty acids.

These products are fundamental ingredients in aquaculture, poultry, and livestock feeds, providing a concentrated source of high-quality protein, energy, minerals, and vitamins for optimal animal growth and health.

We utilize state-of-the-art processing techniques to ensure our Fish Meal boasts superior protein content and minimal histamine levels, while our Fish Oil maintains peak Omega-3 quality and purity.

Applications & Key Benefits

High Biological Value Protein

Contains an exceptionally high concentration of pure animal protein, offering all essential amino acids in an easily digestible form for superior growth efficiency.

Ideal for Pet Food & Treats

Used as a primary, premium protein base in dry and wet pet foods, providing excellent palatability and the necessary nutrients for companion animal health.

Aquaculture Feed Formulation

Its fine particle size and high digestibility make it a perfect component for starter and specialized feed pellets for shrimp and high-value fish species.

Source of Key Minerals

Naturally rich in crucial minerals like Calcium, Phosphorus, and Selenium, supporting skeletal development, immunity, and metabolic functions in farmed animals.
