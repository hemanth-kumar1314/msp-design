Shrimp Soluble Paste

Shrimp Soluble Paste is a high-concentration, semi-liquid feed additive produced by the enzymatic hydrolysis of fresh shrimp by-products. This advanced process breaks down complex proteins into easily digestible peptides and free amino acids.

It functions as an exceptional feed attractant and palatability enhancer, crucial for boosting feed intake, especially in juvenile shrimp and fish, or during periods of stress. Its high content of soluble protein and natural marine compounds ensures maximum nutritional bioavailability.

By utilizing shrimp co-products, we contribute significantly to waste management in the seafood industry, transforming approximately 70% of total shrimp landings that would otherwise become waste into a valuable, sustainable ingredient.

Applications & Key Benefits

Powerful Attractant

Concentrated in free amino acids and peptides, it acts as an irresistible attractant, significantly improving feed acceptance and overall intake in aquatic species.

Rapid Digestibility

As a hydrolysate, its protein is pre-digested, ensuring rapid absorption of nutrients vital for fast growth and energy efficiency in young animals.

Palatability Enhancer

Ideal for incorporating into less palatable or functional feed formulas to enhance taste and smell, supporting consistent, high consumption rates.

Essential Nutrient Source

Provides a rich source of peptides, trace minerals, and vitamins, crucial for immune system development and tissue repair in farmed organisms.
